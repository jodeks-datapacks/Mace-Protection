### Adds the "Mace Protection" Enchantment

Each level reduces the mace smash damage by 4% per armor piece
- Mace Protection I: 4% per armor piece
- Mace Protection II: 8% per armor piece
- Mace Protection III: 12% per armor piece
- Mace Protection IV: 16% per armor piece

**How to obtain:** 
- Has a 8% chance to get it from ominous vaults 

**How to use:**
- can be put on helmets, chestplates, leggings and boots
- Is compatible with any vanilla type of protection
- Is compatible with my "Spear Protection" Enchantment (Upcoming datapack)
